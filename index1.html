Alternating Split

Alternating split is an app that takes every 2nd char from a string, then the other chars,that are not every 2nd char, and concat them as new string. The split is performed over a specified amount of times.

  1. n=1 -> "hsi etTi sats!"
  2. n=2 -> "hsi etTi sats!"-> "s eT ashi tist!"
